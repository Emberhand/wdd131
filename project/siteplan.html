Site Name

"lymanlakeadventures.com" would be the domain name for the site and "Lyman Lake Adventures" would be the name. The name brings up the subject of the lake while making clear that it is a third-party site and not official (not finishing with .gov or .org).

Site Purpose

The site provides information about a camping place in the Uintah mountains, detailing possible activities and campsites for anyone who wishes to stay there.

Scenarios

- What can I do at Lyman Lake?

- What campsites best serve my needs?
